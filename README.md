## ğŸ’¡ Key Findings

### 1. The Wait Time Correlation
Patients are waiting an average of **35.3 minutes**. Data suggests this delay is the primary driver behind the low **4.99/10** Satisfaction Score. Target is missed for **40%** of patients.

### 2. The "Walk-In" Challenge
**60%** of patients (approx 5,400) arrive with **"No Referral"**. These are unexpected walk-ins, making traditional appointment-based staffing ineffective.

### 3. Patient Demographics
- **Admission Rate:** A very high **50%** of visitors are admitted, indicating serious medical cases.
- **Gender:** Slightly higher Male presence (**51.05%**) vs Female (**48.69%**).
- **Peak Traffic:** The busiest days are **Monday (1,377 visits)** and **Saturday (1,322 visits)**.
